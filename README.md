# Road To The Cup

A Football World Cup Qualification and Management Simulator.

## Features

- 211 FIFA Nations
- Full 2026 World Cup Qualification Formats
- Tactical System
- Squad Management
- Training & Development
- Injuries & Suspensions
- Fatigue & Morale
- Live Match Engine
- World Rankings
- Inter-Confederation Playoffs
- 48-Team FIFA World Cup

Created by Nael Al Jamri.
